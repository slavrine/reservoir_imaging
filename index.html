Our tech

We use state-of-the art acquisition and processing technologies.

Standard Borehole Seismic Acquisition

RIL has recorded over one hundred 1D, 2D, 3D and 4D very long array VSP surveys all around the world.

Single or Multi-Well Microseismic Acquisition

RIL pioneered recording long array (80+ levels) Microseismicmonitoring surveys in multiplewellbores and perfecting theprocess over the past 16 years.

Simultaneous Downhole and Surface Acquisition

Our extensive surface anddownhole seismic experience allows RIL to acquired large simultaneous acquisition(s) forthe best subsurface coverage with the shortest most efficientrecording times.

Recoverable Fibre Optic Acquisition

All of RIL’s wirelines included four fibre optic lines allowing for the standard recording as well as DAS recorded data for cross-well stainand production monitoring.

Our Services

VSP Acquisition and Processing

RIL uses long arrays to acquire VSP data efficiently and optimises the recorded data aperture. RIL has processed multiple VSP’s, Offset VSP’s Walkaways and 3D/4D surveys

Single or Multi-Well Microseismic Acquisition

Since the inception of RIL we have developed techniques for quickly deploying extremely long arrays in multiple wells. RIL was the first to successfully use a whip array for monitoring a completion program.

Fibre DAS Acquisition

RIL started working with Fibre optic recording in 2010. Recently we have completed surveys for VSP acquisition, production logging and Microseisimc data recording

Why Us?

Wireline cable has four fibre lines

Our standard wireline cable has six electrical lines and four fibre lines allowing us to record both Geophone data and DAS data using the same cable. For DAS recording two of the fibres are pulled out at the drum and connected to an interrogator. We have worked with a variety of vendors that supply the interrogator so can easily work with your vendor of choice.

Experience Is our strength

Our field crews have work with RIL since the company started 16 years ago. It is this retention that enables us to operate in a very efficient and safe way. Our staff work together to record the optimum data and plan for effective and efficient acquisition.

Health and Safety is a core issue

Over the last 16 years we have developed a comprehensive behavioral based Health and Safety program (RISE) that is rate highly among our clients. RIL is connected to ISNetworld. The RISE program is accessible through our website.

We pride ourselves in working closely with our clients to ensure a safe acquisition.

Flexibility in acquisition

RIL likes to develop bespoke solutions to our clients needs, we are not a cooky cutter company. We have done; simultaneous surface and downhole acquisition, multi wellbore acquisition, simultaneous borehole seismic and Microseismic monitoring. In addition to the option to record DAS data we can also acquire Hydrophone data for a full 4C recording.
